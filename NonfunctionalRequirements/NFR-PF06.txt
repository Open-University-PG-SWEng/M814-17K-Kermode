REQ-ID:PF06-NFR
Description:Property details shall be subject to a consistency and accuracy check prior to being viewed by prospective holidaymakers
Type:Legal Requirement
Rationale:If property details are inaccurate or misleading, the company could be subject to legal action brought by prospective holidaymakers 
Fit Criterion:Property details shall be periodically checked to ensure that the accuracy of stored data has an error rate that exceeds that 
specified by the in-force VisitBritain requirements
