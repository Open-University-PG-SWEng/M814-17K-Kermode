REQ-ID: MM11-NFR
Description: The download feature shall be available for a minimum of 99.9% uptime annually
Type: Performance
Rationale: The download feature should be available so customers can access the property details they need and to avoid reputational risk for the company due to broken features.
Fit Criterion: The download feature should not be unavailable for more than ~44 minutes per month on average to achieve the 99.9% uptime annually.
